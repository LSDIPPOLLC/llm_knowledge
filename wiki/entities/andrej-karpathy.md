# Andrej Karpathy

Co-founder of OpenAI, former AI lead at Tesla, and coined the term "vibe coding."

## Key Contributions to AI Development Paradigms

### Vibe Coding (Feb 2, 2025)
Coined the term on X/Twitter:
> "There's a new kind of coding I call 'vibe coding', where you fully give in to the vibes, embrace exponentials, and forget that the code even exists."

Described his workflow: talking to AI via voice, hitting "Accept All" without reading diffs, pasting error messages back with no comment.

### Agentic Engineering (2026)
Declared vibe coding "passé" and introduced agentic engineering as the evolution:
> "'Agentic engineering': 'agentic' because the new default is that you are not writing the code directly 99% of the time, you are orchestrating agents who do and acting as oversight — 'engineering' to emphasize that there is an art & science and expertise to it."
